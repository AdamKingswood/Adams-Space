Who I Am

I’m someone who enjoys working with people, solving problems, and creating spaces where others can do their best work. My career has taken me through different roles, but the common thread has always been helping teams feel supported, confident, and able to grow.

I'm at my best when I'm helping people solve problems, stay calm under pressure, and find simple ways to move forward. I like creating order, supporting others, and making sure everyone feels included and heard.
